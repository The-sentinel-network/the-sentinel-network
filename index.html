Our Mission

The Sky Belongs to Everyone

SENTINEL is a decentralized, blockchain-anchored network of consumer-grade radar systems designed to monitor, detect, and permanently record aerial phenomena — including UAPs, adversarial drones, and unidentified objects — across the United States and beyond.

Built on the same principles that power the Helium Network, SENTINEL puts the tools of aerial surveillance directly in the hands of ordinary citizens, removing governments, corporations, and even its own founders from the ability to suppress, alter, or control the data it collects.

"Not even the founder of SENTINEL will be able to keep this information away from the people. That is the entire point." — Damon Scott Mayo, Founder

Every detection is cryptographically timestamped, independently verified by multiple nodes, and stored permanently on a public blockchain that no single authority can alter. The network rewards participants with $SNTL tokens, creating a self-sustaining ecosystem where everyday people are paid to watch the sky.

How It Works

Detection Tier System

SENTINEL classifies all detections into four tiers based on their degree of explainability, automatically filtering known aircraft and directing research attention toward genuinely anomalous events.

T1
Fully Identified

Matches ADS-B commercial or private flight data

0.5x Reward
T2
Likely Identified

Consistent with drone, bird, or weather balloon

1x Reward
T3
Anomalous

Speed or maneuver exceeds known aircraft capability

3x Reward
T4
Unresolved

No correlation with any known object or phenomenon

5x Reward
Modular Architecture

Build Your Node, Your Way

SENTINEL is designed from the ground up as a modular system. Every node starts with the Core module and can be expanded with additional sensor modules over time — lowering the barrier to entry while enabling sophisticated multi-sensor detection packages for those who want to go further.

The more sensor modules a node carries, the higher its $SNTL reward multiplier — and the richer the evidence package it produces when something anomalous appears.

📡
Core Module
Required

The foundation of every SENTINEL node. mmWave radar continuously monitors local airspace and submits signed detections to the blockchain.

  • TI IWR6843 mmWave radar (60-64 GHz)
  • Raspberry Pi edge computer
  • Blockchain submission agent
  • ADS-B cross-reference (T1 auto-filter)
  • Geographic zone management
Base Reward: 1x $SNTL
🎙️
Acoustic Module
Optional

Microphone array with AI-powered audio classification. Automatically identifies birds using BirdNET, aircraft engine signatures, and flags unidentified sounds.

  • ReSpeaker microphone array
  • BirdNET AI bird identification
  • Aircraft engine classification
  • Infrasound detection capability
  • Unknown audio flagging
Reward Multiplier: 1.3x $SNTL
📷
Optical Module
Optional

Radar-triggered camera that activates automatically on T2+ detections. Captures photographic evidence and uploads it to IPFS with a blockchain-anchored hash.

  • High-resolution triggered camera
  • Night vision capability
  • Pan/tilt auto-tracking gimbal
  • Auto-upload to IPFS on detection
  • Image hash recorded on blockchain
Reward Multiplier: 1.5x $SNTL
🌡️
Thermal Module
Optional

Infrared thermal imaging triggered alongside the optical camera. Captures heat signatures that optical cameras miss and enables detection in total darkness.

  • FLIR Lepton thermal sensor
  • Heat signature classification
  • Drone vs bird thermal profiling
  • Full night detection capability
  • Thermal anomaly flagging
Reward Multiplier: 1.8x $SNTL
📻
RF Spectrum Module
Optional

Software Defined Radio monitors the electromagnetic spectrum around detected objects. Identifies control frequencies, unknown emissions, and radio silence anomalies.

  • RTL-SDR software defined radio
  • ADS-B signal cross-reference
  • Unknown frequency detection
  • RF emission profiling
  • Radio silence anomaly flagging
Reward Multiplier: 2x $SNTL
🧲
Environmental Module
Optional

Monitors physical environmental changes associated with aerial phenomena including magnetic field disturbances, radiation anomalies, and electromagnetic fluctuations.

  • Magnetometer (magnetic field)
  • Geiger counter (radiation)
  • EMF sensor
  • Barometric pressure sensor
  • Correlated multi-sensor events
Reward Multiplier: 2.2x $SNTL

Full stack node (all modules active) earns up to 3x base $SNTL rewards — and produces the most scientifically compelling evidence packages in the network.

SENTINEL will publish an open Module Standard allowing universities, hobbyists, and companies to develop and certify third-party modules — turning SENTINEL into a platform for aerial monitoring innovation.
