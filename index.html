Our Mission

The Sky Belongs
to Everyone

SENTINEL is a decentralized, blockchain-anchored network of consumer-grade radar systems designed to monitor, detect, and permanently record aerial phenomena — including UAPs, adversarial drones, and unidentified objects — across the United States and beyond.

An American-founded, American-led nonprofit built on the conviction that the data gathered by watching our skies should be owned by no one and available to all. No government agency, no corporation, and not even SENTINEL's own founder can suppress or alter what the network records.

"Not even the founder of SENTINEL will be able to keep this information away from the people. That is the entire point." — Damon Mayo, Founder

Every detection is cryptographically timestamped, independently verified by multiple nodes, and stored permanently on a public blockchain. The network rewards participants with $SNTL tokens — creating a self-sustaining ecosystem where everyday Americans are paid to watch the sky.

How It Works

Detection Tier System

SENTINEL classifies all detections into four tiers based on their degree of explainability — automatically filtering known aircraft and directing research toward genuinely anomalous events. Higher tiers earn higher $SNTL rewards.

T1
Fully Identified

Matches ADS-B commercial or private flight data

0.5× Reward
T2
Likely Identified

Consistent with drone, bird, or weather balloon

1× Reward
T3
Anomalous

Speed or maneuver exceeds known aircraft capability

3× Reward
T4
Unresolved

No correlation with any known object or phenomenon

5× Reward
Modular Architecture

Build Your Node,
Your Way

Every SENTINEL node starts with the Core radar module and expands with optional sensors over time — lowering the barrier to entry while enabling sophisticated multi-sensor detection. More modules means richer data and higher $SNTL rewards.

A full-stack node carrying all sensor modules earns up to 3× base rewards — and produces the most scientifically compelling evidence packages in the network.

📡
Core Module
Required

The foundation of every SENTINEL node. Continuous mmWave radar monitoring with blockchain submission.

  • TI IWR6843 mmWave radar (60-64 GHz)
  • Raspberry Pi edge computer
  • Cryptographic blockchain agent
  • ADS-B auto-classification
Base: 1× $SNTL
🎙️
Acoustic Module
Optional

Microphone array with BirdNET AI — automatically identifies birds to eliminate false positives.

  • ReSpeaker microphone array
  • BirdNET AI bird identification
  • Aircraft engine classification
  • Infrasound detection
Multiplier: 1.3× $SNTL
📷
Optical Module
Optional

Radar-triggered camera activates on T2+ detections. Every image is blockchain-anchored as evidence.

  • High-res triggered camera
  • Night vision capability
  • Pan/tilt auto-tracking gimbal
  • IPFS image upload + hash
Multiplier: 1.5× $SNTL
🌡️
Thermal Module
Optional

FLIR infrared imaging captures heat signatures optical cameras miss. Full night detection capability.

  • FLIR Lepton thermal sensor
  • Heat signature classification
  • Drone vs bird thermal profiling
  • Complete darkness detection
Multiplier: 1.8× $SNTL
📻
RF Spectrum Module
Optional

Software Defined Radio monitors electromagnetic emissions. Flags objects with unknown or absent RF signatures.

  • RTL-SDR software defined radio
  • ADS-B signal cross-reference
  • Unknown frequency detection
  • RF silence anomaly flagging
Multiplier: 2.0× $SNTL
🧲
Environmental Module
Optional

Monitors magnetic fields, radiation, and EMF anomalies correlated with aerial detections.

  • Magnetometer (magnetic field)
  • Geiger counter (radiation)
  • EMF sensor
  • Barometric pressure sensor
Multiplier: 2.2× $SNTL
